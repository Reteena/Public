01
Remembrance Launching in Q3 2026

Remembrance is an emotionally intelligent AI memory companion for Alzheimer's patients that delivers reminiscence therapy through natural conversations. By storing memories in interconnected knowledge graphs that mirror how human memory actually works, Remembrance helps patients maintain their sense of identity and connection to their life stories even as cognitive decline progresses

Seungyong Yang, Sunkalp Chandra, Gautham Korrapati, Dheeraj Chintapalli, Jainish Patel, Noah Small AI, Healthcare, Memory
02
Low Field MRI Framework IEEE BigData 2024 Publication

Low Field MRI Framework enhances low-field MRI scans for Alzheimer’s diagnosis using deep learning-based brain segmentation and volumetric analysis. By enabling 96% diagnostic accuracy on affordable, low-strength scanners, the framework provides a faster, cost-effective alternative to conventional high-field MRI while preserving clinically meaningful structural information for early and accessible neurodegenerative assessment.

Aarav Minocha, Ivan Ma, Jainish Patel, Seungyong Yang Deep Learning, Neuroimaging, Open Source
03
Lark Multi-Stakeholder LLM Agents Published at NEURIPS Workshop 2025

Lark is a biologically inspired decision-making framework that combines LLM-based reasoning with an evolutionary, stakeholder-aware multi-agent system. Through plasticity, duplication and maturation, ranked-choice aggregation, and compute-aware optimization, Lark generates concise, aligned strategies while transparently balancing stakeholder trade-offs, performance, and computational cost across iterative decision cycles.

reteena.com Genomics, Attention, Machine Learning
04
SALSA Speech Analysis Research

SALSA detects Alzheimer's from spontaneous speech using lightweight pre-trained encoders with LoRA adapters, combining acoustic and lexical features for strong cross-dataset performance on consumer hardware.

reteena.com Speech Analysis, AI, Accessibility
05
NeuroMorphLite-fMRI Built on ADNI & OASIS Datasets

NeuroMorphLite-fMRI combines deep learning and reinforcement learning for efficient fMRI-based Alzheimer's research, using CNN and Transformer architectures with RL-optimized regularization for high diagnostic accuracy on low-resource hardware.

reteena.com Neuroimaging, Deep Learning, fMRI
